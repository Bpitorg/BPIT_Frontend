Applied Physics

As we know, Physics provides the solution for the mechanism of the basic phenomena occurring in the field of other sciences. The main aim of the department is to maintain an active, congenial atmosphere for teaching and learning in which faculty members set a high priority in teaching and subscribe to building a caring learning environment for students. The Department has five well qualified and trained teachers who are ready to train and motivate students to inculcate the scientific learning approach among them. Moreover, the department has one well equipped Applied Physics Lab in which the students learn various basic principles of Physics through practice and self realization.
