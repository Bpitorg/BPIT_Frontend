Applied Chemistry

The major concern of department is to familiarize students with the broad concept of Chemistry and the allied fields. The Department engage the students seriously with the environmental issues of day. To this, the department provides broad based and systematic education on the pursuit of “Green Chemistry” a comprehensive approach to the manufacture, management use, disposal and recycling of the chemical substances. There are five well qualified and experienced faculty members to train students in the required fields.
