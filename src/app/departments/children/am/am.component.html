Applied Mathematics

Mathematics is the foundation of all knowledge and sciences. Students having sound fundamentals in mathematics can solve any problem, not only in field of engineering science but also in life. The talented faculty of the department affirm the fundamental principles of mathematics in the students so that they quickly understand and grasp the science of engineering of their chosen Branch.
